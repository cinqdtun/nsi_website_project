The Titan Bot (Archived)

The Titan Bot is a bot specifically designed to streamline the management of item orders on my Discord server dedicated to Paladium, a Minecraft server. This bot has been programmed to allow users to place orders related to obtaining items, equipment, and resources in the game. As an essential tool, The Titan Bot automates the process of managing item orders. With its customizable features, it enables the management of requests, allocation of desired items to players.
